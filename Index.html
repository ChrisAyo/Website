About Us

1990 we begin the journey, our first show, this was not held outdoor,it started in the film club at the university of Greenwich on a colds winters breeze. We held these functions on a monthly basis until the summer came around and it grew,with the sun more friends, students,locals joined and we decided to hold it outside, this was arranged by the art and drama students, we now have annual festivals. Thanks to support from the mayor of Brooklyn and all our sponsors. There are limited seats so ensure you book your tickets today. Looking forward to meet you!
